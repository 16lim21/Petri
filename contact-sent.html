Petri
What is Petri?
Petri is a tool that connect undergraduate students to research opportunities throughout the Columbia University campus. We aggregate all research opportunities across multiple university departments to give students easy access to these opportunities.
How do I use Petri?
First make sure to look through your class schedule and see if you have time to participate in reserach work throughout the semester. Many research opportunities expect students to commit to an agreed upon set of hours each week so don’t agree to commit to something you don’t have time for!

Then, look through all the opportunities posted on Petri. Use our search and filter features to narrow down the experiences to only those that interest you. Don’t forget to read through the descriptions for each opportunity to get a better understanding of what you would be doing.

Once you have identified a few suitable opportunities, click on the opportunity’s “contact” button to send the researchers an email through our website. Make sure to include any information they listed in the “What we want to know about you” section. Once an inqury is submitted, researchers will respond directly to your email.
How do I keep track of positions I have applied for?
Click on the “My Account” button in the top right to look at opportunities you have applied to.
Additional information
The biology department has written a great guide to finding research opportunities at Columbia. It includes other information such as how to structure your emails or information on researching for credit. Make sure to check out the resource here.
